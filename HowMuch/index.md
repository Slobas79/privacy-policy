# Privacy Policy for HowMuch

**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026

HowMuch ("the App") is developed by Slobodan Stamenic ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

## Data Collection
HowMuch does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App works fully offline and does not require an account.

## Local Storage
The App stores your language preferences (selected source and target languages) locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

## Third-Party Services
HowMuch does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or SDKs.

## Children's Privacy
HowMuch does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Permissions
The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or any other sensitive device data.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does HowMuch work offline?
Yes. All phrases, translations, and pronunciations work without an internet connection.

### How do I change my language?
Open the **Languages** tab at the bottom of the screen and select your source (spoken) and target (learning) language.

### Why can't I hear the pronunciation?
HowMuch uses your device's built-in text-to-speech. Make sure:
- Your device volume is turned up and not on silent
- The target language voice is installed: **Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices**

### Which languages are supported?
HowMuch supports 50+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, and many more.

### Does HowMuch collect my data?
No. HowMuch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. See our [Privacy Policy](./) for details.

### How much does HowMuch cost?
HowMuch is free to download and use.
